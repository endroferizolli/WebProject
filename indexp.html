Meet the Twilight creator

"I like the night. Without the dark, we'd never see the stars." - Stephenie Meyer

Stephenie Meyer is born December 24, 1973 .
She is an American novelist and film producer.
She is best known for writing the vampire romance
series Twilight, which has sold over 160 million copies,
with translations into 37 different languages. Meyer was
the bestselling author of 2008 and 2009 in the U.S., having
sold over 29 million books in 2008 and 26.5 million in 2009.
Meyer received the 2009 Children's Book of the Year award from
the British Book Awards for Breaking Dawn.
